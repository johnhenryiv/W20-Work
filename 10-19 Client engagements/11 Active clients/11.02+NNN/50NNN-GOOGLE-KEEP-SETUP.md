# 50NNN Launch — Google Keep Setup

## Quick Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create a Shared Notebook
1. Go to **keep.google.com**
2. Click **Create new note** → select **New notebook**
3. Name it: `50NNN Launch`
4. Click the three dots → **Share**
5. Add Eric's email — give him **Editor** access
6. Click **Share**

Done. Now you both have a living space for the project.

---

## How to Organize Your Notebook

Use **labels** (like folders) to keep things organized. Create these labels:

- **📋 MASTER STATUS** — Project overview, deadlines, launch timeline
- **🎙️ PODCAST** — Episode ideas, guest host names, topics
- **📹 SEGMENTS** — "50NNN" scripted ideas, quick-story themes
- **🤝 PARTNERSHIPS** — OR9, SET app, 24W notes
- **👥 CONTACTS** — Guest hosts, talent, collaborators
- **💡 IDEAS** — Brainstorms, funny moments, inspiration
- **📅 CALENDAR** — Weekly tasks, deadlines, reminders
- **✅ CHECKLIST** — Action items for you and Eric

## Sharing with Eric

**What to share:**
- Just the Google Keep notebook — that's it
- He can see everything, add notes, and stay in the loop

**What NOT to share (keep private):**
- Any personal notes about finances or sensitive partnerships
- Your Bitwarden account or Synology password
- Other sensitive projects
